American Guild of Organist
Portland Oregon Chapter

We're in our 104th year!

The Portland, Oregon, Chapter encompasses organists, church music directors, and organ aficionados along the Columbia River and northern Willamette Valley in Oregon and southern Washington. Locales include (but are not limited to) Astoria, McMinnville, Scappoose, Wilsonville, Pendleton, Bend, Vancouver, and the greater Portland area, with a couple of dual-members coming from Salem. Membership in the Guild is done through our national office through its ONCARD system.
