AGO EVENTS FOR THE 2014-15 SEASON:

Rodgers Factory Tour ~ Wednesday, June 10 ~ 10:30am

The gracious staff at Rodgers Instruments will be giving AGO members an exclusive tour of the factory floor during production. After the tour, we will have an opportunity to play any of the models on display in their demonstration room, so bring your shoes. Afterwards, lunch will be provided; please notify Karl Jurisons if you plan on attending lunch. Rodgers Instruments (map) is located at 1300 NE 25th Ave in Hillsboro,OR.

Monthly Meetings

The chapter's Executive Committee normally meets monthly on second Tuesdays at 7:00pm at the current Dean's church or residence.

There is also a general membership, non-business meeting monthly on the last Tuesday of each month at 11:30am at the Pastini Pastaria (map) is located at 1426 NE Broadway St. Portland, OR. Free parking can be found in a garage on 14th Avenue between Broadway & Weidler. There is no reservation at the restaurant; the first one arriving arranges tables. All members and guests are invited to come enjoy good food and conversation, some organ-related, some not. The meals are Dutch-style.
