Jobs

Are you an AGO member who is available to substitute? Have you moved into Portland and are looking for open positions? For currently available post please refer to the current edition of our newsletter The Prestant.

Currently, our job placements coordinator is an open position. Until a new person fills this role, please send job opening details to Heidi Kohne. Members who are available to substitute or who are needing substitutes should refer to their directory.

The American Guild of Organists has produced a national guideline for compensation for church musicians. 2015 Salary Guide

The Portland chapter has also produced local guidelines for compensation. Republished in 2002, these guidelines are based upon national's guidelines along with Portland's standard of living index. If you would like to request a booklet, please notify Heidi Kohne . Please note that there is a $2.50 charge for non-members to cover printing expenses and postage.
