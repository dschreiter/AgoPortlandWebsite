Scholarships

The Portland, Oregon chapter has established a scholarship fund and is now accepting applications! These funds are specifically to support the study of the King of Instruments and have come from generous individual donors, members, and proceeds from our Bachathons. There are restrictions, which you can find on the application form, but these funds are available to people of any age.

    APPLICATION FORM

We hope that you will pursue your studies and apply for these scholarship funds.
Many thanks go to Grant Edwards for working hard to make this a reality.
